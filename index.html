Meet the Author

Hi, I'm Max

I create books that bring joy, spark creativity, and make meaningful moments — whether that's a toddler discovering their favorite colors, a child lost in an imaginative world, or a woman deepening her spiritual practice through guided journaling.

Every book in the BooksByMax Designs catalog is crafted with intention: thoughtful layouts, quality content, and a genuine desire to add something valuable to your bookshelf. From my first dinosaur coloring book to guided prayer journals and family recipe books, each title reflects a passion for creative, purposeful design.

With thousands of copies sold across four Amazon marketplaces, I'm grateful for every reader who has welcomed a BooksByMax title into their home. New books are always in the works — follow along to see what's next!
